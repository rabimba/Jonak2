A Little About Me

I'm Jonak. I have a passion for speed and an eye for beauty. Beauty, I like to think, lies in the eyes of the beholder. Beautiful things can be found in the unlikeliest places, amidst our everyday ordinariness.

I seek them out, I hunt them down, I capture and worship them with my lens. My website is an archive of such beautiful moments, seen and frozen forever in time through the eye of my camera.
